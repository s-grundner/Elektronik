# Reflexionsfaktor

![](assets/TL_QundS.png)

> $$ r = \frac{U_{r}}{U_{h}} = \frac{\frac{1}{2}(U_{0}-I_{0}\cdot Z_{L})}{\frac{1}{2}(U_{0}+I_{0}\cdot Z_{L})} = \frac{\frac{U_{0}}{I_{0}}-Z_{L}}{\frac{U_{0}}{I_{0}}+Z_{L}}$$
