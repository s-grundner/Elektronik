# Arithmetische Reihe

$$
\begin{align}
a_{n+1}-a_{n} &=d \\
a_{n} &= a_{1} + (n-1)\cdot d \\
a_{n} &= \frac{1}{2}\cdot (a_{n+1}+a_{n-1}) \\
s_{n} &= \frac{n}{2} \cdot (2\cdot a_{1}+(n-1)\cdot d) \\
&=\frac{n}{2} \cdot (a_{1}+a_{n})
\end{align}
$$
