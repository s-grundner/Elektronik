# Sinus Cardinalis

> [!def] **D1 - SINC)** Sinus Cardinalis
> $$ \operatorname{sinc}(x) = \frac{\sin x}{x}$$


> [!def] **D2 - SINC)** Normalisierter Sinus Cardinalis
> $$ \operatorname{sinc}(x) = \frac{\sin (\pi x)}{\pi x}$$
