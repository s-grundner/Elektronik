### Herleitung

**Knotenregel:**

$$
\begin{align}
\frac{U_{e}}{R} + \frac{U_{a}}{Z_{C}} = 0\iff\frac{U_{e}}{R} =- \frac{U_{a}}{Z_{C}} \iff \frac{Z_{C}}{R}= -\frac{U_{a}}{U_{e}} = -\frac{1}{j\omega R C}
\end{align}
$$
